Fermented Tea:

pu'erh & etc.

description:

(post)fermented tea, or dark tea, is a remnant of the 1st age of tea
when it was boiled and prepared with a mixture of other ingredients, as
more of a medicine or tonic than a refined beverage of idle enjoyment.
This classic preparation summons forth tea's original roots.

Ingredients:

  • brick or cake tea of choice
  • roasted rice
  • ginger root
  • rock salt
  • orange peel
  • spices
  • yak milk
  • onion of choice

Tools:

  • iron kettle
  • tripod brazier
  • tea untensil cabinet
  • tea utensils of choice

Steps:

  1. prepare the tea according to its individual character
  2. serve the tea with care and grace
  3. dream of evanescance
  4. linger in the beautiful foolishness of things

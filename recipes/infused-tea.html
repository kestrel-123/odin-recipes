Infused Tea:

loose-leaf, & etc.

Description:

Infused whole-leaf tea is where we are now, in the 3rd age of tea. This
is how most people know make tea, a portion of leaves are steeped in water for a
desired length of time, before being removed entirely so that the infused water can be
served or drank as a beverage of it's own.

Ingredients:

  • loose-leaf tea of choice
  • fresh spring or mineral water

Tools:

  • iron kettle
  • infusing basket
  • measuring tool (optional)

Steps:

  1. prepare the tea according to its individual character
  2. serve the tea with care & grace
  3. imbibe
  4. dream of evanescence
  5. linger in the beautiful foolishness of things

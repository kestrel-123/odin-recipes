Powdered Tea:

matcha & etc.

Description:

Powdered tea is a remnant of the 2nd age of tea when stone-ground tea leaf
was whisked in water to make a vibrant, frothy bowl of tea. It was in this era that
tea shifted from use as a medicinal ingredient to an enjoyable beverage in its own right.
Here tea's potential blossomed into the realms of culture & ceremony. This preparation is
scaffolding from which a personal tea ceremony can be constructed.

Ingredients:

  • finely ground tea leaf of choice
  • fresh spring or mineral water

Tools:

  • iron kettle
  • cut bamboo whisk
  • tea scoop
  • tea bowl
  • tea towel

Steps:

  1. prepare the tea according to its individual character
  2. serve the tea with care & grace
  3. imbibe
  4. dream of evanescence
  5. linger in the beautiful foolishness of things
